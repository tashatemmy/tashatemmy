About Me

I’m Temi Odey, a motivated Software QA Engineer with hands-on experience in manual and automation testing. I’m passionate about ensuring that every product delivers a seamless and trustworthy user experience.

My journey into QA is fueled by curiosity, problem-solving, and continuous learning. With a background in psychology, I bring a unique perspective to testing—understanding not just how systems work, but also how real users think, behave, and interact with software.

🔹 What I Do Best

  • ✔ Defect Detection & Root Cause Analysis – Skilled at identifying critical defects early and tracing them back to requirements or code, saving teams time and cost.
  • ✔ Critical & Analytical Thinking – Imploring logic and psychology-driven observation to analyze user behavior, anticipate edge cases, and test beyond the obvious.
  • ✔ End-to-End Test Coverage – Designing and executing manual/automated test cases that ensure functional, regression, performance, and usability coverage.
  • ✔ Automation Excellence - Building scalable, maintainable automation frameworks (Selenium, TestNG, POM, Maven) with CI/CD integration (Jenkins, GitHub Actions).
  • ✔ User-Centric Quality Assurance - Applying empathy and psychology background to test with the end-user in mind, ensuring intuitive and seamless experiences.
  • ✔ Effective Communication & Collaboration - Bridging gaps between developers, product managers, and stakeholders with clear test reports and defect documentation.
  • ✔ Process Improvement & Reliability – Advocating for QA best practices, implementing RTMs, and improving workflows that strengthen release confidence.
  • ✔ Risk-Based Testing – Prioritizing testing efforts on high-impact areas to deliver maximum quality within project timelines.
  • ✔ Adaptility & Continuous Learning - Quickly learning new tools, frameworks, and environments to align with evolving project needs.
